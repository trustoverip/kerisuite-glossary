[[def: proof-id, Proof-ID]] 

~ is the Self-Addressing Identifier (SAID) of a cryptographic proof object that uses CESR-Paths to reference specific fields within a data structure, enabling selective disclosure and verifiable provenance of those fields.

~ More info: CESR-Paths are used to extract specific pieces of data from a container (like an [[ref: ACDC]]).
